The Catalogue

Experiences for every kind of team

Every session happens at your office premises. Pick a category, pick an experience — Sowaka handles everything else.

🏆

Circuit Challenge

A week-long fitness programme where teams compete across daily workouts, fitness tasks, and stretching streaks, culminating in a relay-style obstacle race inspired by Hyrox and Devil Circuit. Guaranteed high point for every employee — fit or not.

⭐ Most Popular
🎉

Office Olympics

The floor is split into teams competing across physical challenges. A great way to get everyone moving — and an even better opportunity for genuine team bonding.

💃

Grooves — Zumba & Dance

Sometimes Masala Bhangra, sometimes Bollywood fusion, sometimes team dance-offs between bays. Always creative, always inclusive. Maximum fun on the office floor.

🥊

Self Defense & Kickboxing

Self defense is about escape mindset, not fighting. The kickboxing coach brings all equipment — real kicks, real punches, real stress relief. Especially great for women.

🧘

Yoga & Meditation

Sessions include guided meditation, face yoga, and breathwork — highly customised based on job roles, gamified on request. Addresses sleep issues, anxiety, energy crashes, spine pain, eye strain, and wrist strain. Proven and powerful.

🌟 Team Favourite
🎶

Sound Therapy

Singing bowls and chimes guide the brain into a state of no thoughts. Extremely relaxing after high-pressure periods. The perfect introduction to meditation for beginners.

💚

Emotional Wellbeing

Not a generic stress lecture. Customised to your team's specific context and stressors — employees learn to recognise negative emotions and build practical coping techniques.

🪑

Ergonomics

A physio-led session blending tips, games, and stretches. Essential relief for the cumulative physical toll of 8+ hours of sitting at a desk every single day.

🎨

Creative Expression — Art Workshop

Teams paint bags, mugs, and coasters they take home or display at their desks. Time off screens, creative brain engagement, and the dopamine of completing something beautiful — all proven to meaningfully reduce stress levels.

🎭 Most Requested
🏺

Pottery Workshop

Guided wheel pottery — the experience of building something from clay with your own hands is deeply rewarding and unlike any other office activity your team has tried.

🌱

Gardening & Soil Therapy

Hands in the earth, close to nature. One of the most calming and grounding experiences available — and employees leave with something alive to care for at their desks.

🤲

Clay Sculpting

A tactile, meditative group session where teams create clay sculptures. No experience needed — just willingness to get hands messy and minds quiet.

💰

Financial Planning Workshop

Actionable, not just gyaan. Delivered with a hands-on Excel tool, breaking financial planning into four clear steps: insurance, tracking inflow and outflow, setting objectives, and investing. Directly addresses the universal stressor — money.

🔥 High Impact
🔬

Myth Buster Polling

Health topics — nutrition, skincare, PCOS, diabetes, hypothyroidism — turned into live interactive polling. Cuts through marketing gimmicks and empowers real decisions.

🩺

Body Scans & Check-ups

BCA scans, BP check-ups, and annual health check-ups done at your office. Nothing triggers action and healthy behaviour change like a red flag on your own reports.

📈

Workplace Effectiveness

Communication, collaboration, influence, and leadership — through experiential learning only. No lectures. Includes procrastination and motivation assessments to boost productivity.
